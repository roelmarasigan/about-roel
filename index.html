About Rogelio Marasigan

Also known as "Roel," our hero was Born in Los Angeles, California in June of 1989.

He bravely attended community college before transferring to the luxurious University of Hawaii. Roel graduated in the autumn of 2015 with a Bachelor's Degree.

After graduation, Roel worked at a credit union in the loan department, heroically assisting members with their financial needs.

Roel's family purchased a haunted motel property in Holbrook, Arizona in 2018, and began frightening guests who used the mini fridge.

Currently, Roel reside in Eastern New Mexico, assisting with another small 35-room Ghost Hotel on historic Route 66.

In order to fight the ghosts and protect the guests, Roel trained in Jiu-Jitsu. He served as a walk-on for his college basketball team, and enjoys camping in the most obvious place: outdoors!
